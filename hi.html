Leonardo da Vinci

Velmora art & design is my passion

Leonardo da Vinci was a true Renaissance genius, excelling as a painter, scientist, inventor, and thinker. Born in 1452 in Italy, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest artists of all time. His masterpieces, including the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper, showcase his extraordinary skill in capturing human emotion and detail. Beyond art, Leonardo studied anatomy, engineering, and nature, blending science with creativity. His notebooks reveal ideas centuries ahead of his time, from flying machines to detailed anatomical sketches. Leonardo’s curiosity and relentless pursuit of knowledge made him a pioneer in multiple fields. He believed art and science were deeply connected, each enhancing the other. His timeless masterpieces and visionary ideas continue to captivate and inspire generations.
